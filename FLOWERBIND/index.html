Welcome to FLOWERBIND

Step into the mysterious Greenhouse as a human teen trapped in a world of Demons and ancient beings. Inspired by classic RPGs, Cave Story, and anime, FLOWERBIND challenges you to navigate complex relationships where your choices matter - but not everything can be changed. Will you fight or befriend? The fate of the Greenhouse awaits your decision.

About FLOWERBIND

FLOWERBIND is an original indie RPG inspired by classic RPGs, Cave Story, anime, and more. You play as a human teen who finds themselves trapped in a mysterious giant tree called the Greenhouse - a strange land filled with secrets and danger.

The Greenhouse is inhabited by beings called Demons who harbor deep resentment toward humans. These Demons were sealed away in the Greenhouse by humans for unknown reasons, alongside other mysterious beings that existed before them.

Interact with the inhabitants of the Greenhouse - fight or befriend them, your choices matter! But remember: you cannot decide everyone's fate. Some stronger foes will stand against you, and certain destinies cannot be changed no matter how hard you try.

Game Features

  • Team system (Party Member mechanics)
  • Action-based combat with dodge mechanics
  • Fight or befriend system with consequences
  • Multiple combat options and strategies
  • Moral choices that shape your journey
  • Immutable fate system - some things cannot be changed
  • Explore the mysterious Greenhouse world
  • Interact with Demons and ancient beings
  • Original storyline inspired by multiple sources
